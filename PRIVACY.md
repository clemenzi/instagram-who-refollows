# Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 11, 2026

"Who refollows me?" (the "Extension") is a browser extension developed by Valerio Clemenzi. It helps you identify Instagram profiles you follow that do not follow you back.

## Summary

The Extension runs locally in your browser while you are logged in to Instagram. It does not include a backend server, does not create user accounts, and does not send your Instagram data to the developer.

## Data the Extension Reads

When you start a scan, the Extension reads information from Instagram that is necessary to compare your followers and following lists, including:

- Instagram usernames
- Display names
- Profile picture URLs
- Follower and following relationships for the profile being analyzed

This information is used only to calculate and display the scan results in your browser.

## How Data Is Processed

All scan logic runs inside your browser. The Extension requests data from Instagram using your existing Instagram session and compares the followers and following lists locally.

The Extension does not:

- Collect analytics
- Track your browsing activity outside Instagram
- Sell or share personal data
- Upload scan results to a remote server
- Store your Instagram data in a developer-controlled database

## Storage

The Extension does not intentionally store your scan results remotely. Results are displayed in the Instagram page where the Extension is running and are available only during your local browser session.

## Third Parties

The Extension communicates with Instagram only to retrieve the information needed for the scan. Your use of Instagram remains subject to Instagram's own terms and privacy policy.

The developer does not receive, control, or process the data returned by Instagram during a scan.

## Permissions

The Extension runs on Instagram pages so it can add its user interface and perform the scan when you request it. These permissions are used only for the Extension's stated purpose.

## Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if the Extension changes how it handles data. Any updates will be reflected in this file.

## Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact the developer through the repository or distribution page where you obtained the Extension.
